美到爆炸的英文短句

2024-10-25

Do or die.

生死攸关

Now or never.

千载难逢

Swim or drown.

破釜沉舟

Love is blind.

爱情使人盲目

Take it or leave it.

态度坚定

Close, but no cigar;

功亏一篑

Catch as catch can.

竭尽全力

Might makes right.

强者为王

Rise from the ashes.

浴火重生

Go big or go home.

全力以赴

Let them eat cake.

何不食肉糜

Live and learn.

吃一堑，长一智

Man is mortal.

人生自古谁无死

Still waters run deep.

静水流深

Fish for compliments.

沽名钓誉

Conquer from within.

战胜自我

Bring home the bacon.

养家糊口

Years bring wisdom.

年长则智增

Look before you leap.

三思而后行

Rumor is a lying jade.

流言不可信

Good things come slow.

好事多磨

Every day is not Sunday.

好景不长

Knowledge is power

知识就是力量

Turn up like a bad penny.

阴魂不散

Carrot and stick

恩威并用，软硬兼施

Live to fight another day.

不屈不挠

I will not forget who I am.

不忘初心

Practise what you preach.

言行如一

Pride comes before a fall.

骄兵必败

Win or bust.

志在必得

Use it or lose it.

用进废退

Divide and rule.

分而治之

Go with your gut.

相信直觉

Seize the moment.

只争朝夕

The ship has sailed.

木已成丹

Time is money.

时间就是金钱

All we have is now.

只争朝夕

Every minute counts.

分秒必争

You had me at hello.

一见钟情

Do a good turn daily.

日行一善

Patience is a virtue.

忍耐是美德

Trials and tribulations

艰辛磨难

Have nothing to lose.

无所畏惧

Much will have more.

欲壑难填

Easier said than done.

知易行难

Every man to his taste.

见仁见智

First come, first served.

先到先得

Love sees no faults.

爱情是盲目的

Every dog has its day.

风水轮流转

The best is yet to come.

未来可期

Harmony brings wealth.

和气生财

The die is cast.

木已成舟，覆水难收

All or nothing.

孤注一掷放手一搏

Leave no stone unturned.

不遗余力

A cat may look at a king.

众生平等

Hunger is the best sauce.

饥不择食

Make or break.

成王败寇，生死存亡

Let’s call a spade a spade.

实事求是

More haste, less speed.

欲速则不达
